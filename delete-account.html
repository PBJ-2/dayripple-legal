Delete your DayRipple account and data

How to delete the data linked to your DayRipple account.

Delete instantly in the app

Sign in to DayRipple, then tap Settings → Account → Delete account. After you confirm, deletion begins immediately.

If you cannot access the app

From the email address you signed up with, send a deletion request using the button below. Do not send your password or payment card number.

Request deletion by email

Email: ehxhfl92@gmail.com

Data that is deleted

  • Your login account, email, profile, and nickname
  • Space memberships and push notification tokens
  • Device-side pending-purchase target info, scheduled notifications, and analytics-identifier state
  • Events, anniversaries / D-days, and to-dos you created
  • Spaces you used alone and their data

Shared spaces where other members remain are transferred to those members. Payment records with a legal retention obligation may be stored separately for the statutory period, and a shared space’s purchase entitlement may remain with its link to the deleted account removed. Anonymous product statistics that are aggregated without account linkage are not re-identified as any specific account’s data. If you need existing Sentry error reports deleted, please note that in your email request.

Email requests are processed promptly after identity verification, normally completed within 7 days.
